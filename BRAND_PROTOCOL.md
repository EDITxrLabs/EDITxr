### 2. Ecosystem Rights: EDITxrVerse™
The **EDITxrVerse™** is the official designation for the spatial ecosystem and decentralized environments built upon the EDITxr™ framework. All rights to the name, conceptual architecture, and environment-editing protocols of the EDITxrVerse™ are strictly reserved.

### 3. Organizational Structure
**EDITxrLabs** serves as the internal research and development entity. While EDITxrLabs manages the core development, the primary industry-facing identity remains **EDITxr™**.

### 4. Open-Source Integration & Proprietary R&D

*No part of the EDITxr™ framework or EDITxrVerse™ protocols may be utilized for commercial purposes without an official licensing agreement.*
